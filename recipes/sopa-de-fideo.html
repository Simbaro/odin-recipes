Sopa De Fideo

Description

Fideo soup is what Mexican childhood memories are made of. It is a warm comforting soup made with a tangy tomato base and golden brown noodles.

Ingrediants

  • Pasta fideo packet
  • Knorr Granulated Chicken Bouillon Seasoning
  • canned tomato sauce
  • salt

Steps

  1. Place entire fideo packet in pan and let simmer while stirring occasionally until noodels turn brown
  2. add 2 cups of water and continue stirring until water is boiling
  3. add salt and spoonful of knorr seasoning and continue stirring
  4. taste if right and let it simmer till noodles are soggy enough to eat
